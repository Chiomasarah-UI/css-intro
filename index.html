MY LOVE FOR TECHNOLOGY

my name is Abana chioma,While growing up I was exposed to the newest technologies of cell phones that came into my family’s cellphone store in Nigeria where I was raised. Regardless of my low age, I was able to quickly understand and gain a passion for technology. My family did not only sell phones but they also repaired them and provided tech support for customers. As a six year old kid, I would learn from my older brothers all they knew about current technologies. Thanks to the background that I was raised in, I was able to create an interest for all kinds of technology. I would spend countless hours looking at the components of the broken phones and replace them with working parts. My family would then give me a part of the profit of the phones I repaired. This made me realize that I could make a living as a computer engineer. As I continued my education, I was not given many opportunities to develop my skills as a technician but all the chances that I got I valued them and took full advantage of the opportunities offered. Due to this lack of support through the public education system I was forced to learn and continue developing my passion on my own free time. After my university, I decided to learn more about programming. I still continue to have my passion for technology and love sharing my knowledge about it with my friends. I thank my parents for providing me with a background in technology that not many have had the luck to have. I will continue to grow on my passion for technology as I go off to college. I hope to someday have the materials to experiment and express myself in a creative technologic environment.

What Is CSS?
CSS stands for cascading style sheets. In short, CSS is a design language that makes a website look more appealing than just plain or uninspiring pieces of text. Whereas HTML largely determines textual content, CSS determines visual structure, layout, and aesthetics. HTML is a markup language, and CSS is a style sheet language. Think “look and feel” when you think CSS.
How Does CSS Work with HTML?
If HTML were the engine components of a car, CSS would be the body style and the paint job. A website can run without CSS, but it certainly isn’t pretty. CSS makes the front-end of a website shine and it creates a great user experience. Without CSS, websites would be less pleasing to the eye and likely much harder to navigate. In addition to layout and format, CSS is responsible for font color and more.

DIFFERENCE BETWEEN MARGINS,PADDINGS & BORDERS

In CSS, a margin is the space around an element’s border and padding is the space between an element’s border and the element’s content while Border is the perimeter of the box. Borders can be invisible or they could be assigned colors to make them visible. Put another way, the margin property controls the space outside an element, and the padding property controls the space inside an element while the border sorrounds the element kinda like a fence.
